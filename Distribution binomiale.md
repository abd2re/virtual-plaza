# Distribution binomiale
- Notation:: $X\sim B(n,P)$
<!--SR:!2023-10-18,1,230-->
- $P(X=x)$=::${\binom n x}p^{x}(1-p)^{x}$      
<!--SR:!2023-10-18,1,230-->
- $E(x)$=::$np$
<!--SR:!2023-10-19,2,230-->
- $\text{var}(x)$=::$E(x)(1-p)=np(1-p)$
<!--SR:!2023-10-27,10,250-->
