$$\int \sqrt{1+x^{2}}\,dx$$
$u=\sqrt{1+x^{2}},\;\frac{du}{dx}=\frac{x}{\sqrt{1+x^{2}}}$
$\frac{dv}{dx}=1,\;v=x$
$$\int uv\,dx=uv-\int\frac{du}{dx}v\,dx$$

$$\int \sqrt{1+x^{2}}\,dx=x\sqrt{1+x^{2}}-\int \frac{x^{2}}{\sqrt{1+x^{2}}}\,dx$$
$$\int \sqrt{1+x^{2}}\,dx=x\sqrt{1+x^{2}}-\int \frac{x^{2}\sqrt{1+x^2}}{1+x^{2}}\,dx$$
